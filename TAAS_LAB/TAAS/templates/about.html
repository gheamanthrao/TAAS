About Us


This TAAS Weiste is a data management system aimed at handling all the data of a car rental company. The purpose of the project is to make data handling a very easy and efficient, to help the agency in organising and analysing the data of all cars and customers. The data can be of cars or of customers. It is an alternative to the conventional hard copy management of all the data. The data can be changed, deleted or added anytime, such as a new car can be added to the travel agency, a car can be rented, car can be sent to repair etc. This data can be managed using a software. So, the website ”TAAS” is a way to achieve this. This helps in automation of listing of the cars present to give for renting, calculate the cost for renting, give statistic etc.


Advantages of TAAS:


  • Common place for administrator and customers
  • More control over the processes in agency
  • Ease of use and time-efficient
  • Reduced risks of human errors
  • Easy sales analysis
  • Suggestions for price of rent to increase productivity
  • Quick, easy and secure retrieval of information
